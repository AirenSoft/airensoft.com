OvenMediaEngine Enterprise

The Tailored Live Streaming Server, built to be fine-tuned for your needs

OvenMediaEngine Enterprise is a Sub-Second Latency Streaming Server for Enterprises, built on the OvenMediaEngine. Because it is installable software that runs in your own environment, you can fine-tune everything from ingest and transcoding to delivery and monitoring. Instead of adapting to a one-size-fits-all service, you can shape a streaming service that fits your exact requirements, backed by commercial licensing, guaranteed long-term support, customization, an easy-to-deploy installation package, advanced security, monetization, and more enterprise-grade features.

FAQ
OvenMediaEngine Enterprise includes the OpenH264 as a software encoder and GPU encoders from NVIDIA and AMD Xilinx by default. You may scale usage without a channel cap as long as your server capacity allows.
Both encode H.264. OpenH264 is great for real-time and lightweight use, while x264 delivers higher quality at the same bitrate (or the same quality at a lower bitrate) thanks to advanced encoding tools and presets.
We officially support x264 commercial licensing for OvenMediaEngine Enterprise, so you can ship closed-source, enterprise-grade services with improved compression efficiency.
Billing is per channel. One channel means one x264-encoded output video track. A single live stream can include multiple video, audio, and subtitle tracks; therefore, if it has five x264-encoded output video tracks, it counts as five channels. For example, if you ingest a media source into OvenMediaEngine Enterprise and encode an ABR stream with x264 producing three output video tracks (1080p, 720p, 480p), that counts as three channels.
You can mix OpenH264, x264, and GPU encoders from NVIDIA and AMD Xilinx to meet your workload and quality targets. For example, keep 1080p on the GPU while using x264 for premium profiles. We help you select presets to meet your quality and latency targets, and you can offload other profiles to balance resources.

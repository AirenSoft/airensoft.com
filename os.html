Advantages

Low Latency Streaming

OvenStudio LLHLS supports Low Latency HLS (LLHLS) streaming, delivering low latency video/audio playback to viewers.

Web Console

OvenStudio LLHLS provides a user-friendly web console, offering an intuitive and easy-to-use GUI for efficient management and monitoring of streaming workflows.

Adaptive Bitrate

Is a CDN connection really necessary?

No, a CDN connection is not mandatory as the primary server (cloud) hosting OvenStudio LLHLS is sufficient for delivering low-latency streaming. However, if you have a large audience reaching millions or tens of millions, connecting a CDN can enhance the reliability of stream distribution. With OvenStudio LLHLS, integrating a CDN is effortless, allowing you to expand your streaming service seamlessly with just a few clicks.

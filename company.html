Our Team and Philosophy

Open-Source Team

The Open-Source Team drives the development of OvenMediaEngine, a sub-second latency streaming server used and trusted worldwide.

  • Ship and refine the OvenMediaEngine core in public.
  • Support the community through GitHub, email, and docs.
  • Share what we learn with blogs, tutorials, and live demos.

Enterprise Team

The Enterprise Team takes the OvenMediaEngine core and turns it into hard-mode streaming platforms for mission-critical businesses.

  • Deliver sub-second latency with guaranteed long-term support.
  • Build custom features around specific business needs.
  • Provide direct access to core developers for design and operation.

Limitless Connectivity

We break down the barriers of time and space. Sub-second latency brings people closer, enabling real-time interaction as if they were in the same room.

Open Innovation

We grow with the community. By opening our core engine to the world, we evolve faster through collective intelligence.

Reliability First

Media services are mission-critical. We obsess over stability, offering enterprise-grade support and high-availability architecture.
